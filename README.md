## 2. Basic operations
   Keyboard shortcuts:
      1 ---  Fourth prize
      2 ---  Third prize
      3 ---  Second prize
      4 ---  First prize
      5 ---  Special prize
      6 ---  Lucky Boss
      7 ---  Lucky Star
      8 ---  Lucky actor
      SPACE  ---  Start/Stop
      R ---  RESET
      ENTER --- Toggle fullscreen(browser)
      T ---  Destroy toggleJumpscare
      BACKSPACE --- Delete last chosen candidate(Undo)
